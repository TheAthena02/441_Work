What do I see?

I see a world at war with itself. Everyone is against everyone. Theres conflict everwhere, a conflict that is heightened by algarithms, news cycles, and egos. But I also see the kindness that springs up when the war is forgetten, like the soldiers of WWI playing football in no mans land during christmas day. I see that people still have compassion and love, when there isn't an insentive to hurt and fight. I want to help remove those insentives and encourage kindness between people. I hope that I can create games that help do this.
